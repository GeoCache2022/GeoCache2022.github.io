How it works

1. You found a geocache and scanned the qr-code, what now?

To open the treasure, you will need to answer a few question first about chess



2. Running the geocache

Find a way to turn the geocache on. When turned on the display will flash and the timer
will start



3. Start the quiz

Click the button at the bottom of the page to have a peek at the questions.



4. Enter the answers

Use the buttons on the geocache to select the correct answers. Correct answers will
switch on an LED light
